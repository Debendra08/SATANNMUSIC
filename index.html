SATANN began his musical journey in 2021, turning real-life emotions like love, heartbreak, and struggle into music. Over time, he evolved his sound and storytelling, creating tracks like Aaina and Eyes that connect deeply with listeners. Today, SATANN represents growth and “enlightenment after darkness,” reflecting his journey from raw beginnings to a more refined artist.
